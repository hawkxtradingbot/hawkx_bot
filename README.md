## 🎯 What is HawkX?

HawkX is a **non-custodial Solana trading bot** built natively inside Telegram. No websites, no browser extensions — just fast, secure trading from your phone.

**Built for:** Solana traders who want speed, security, and full control of their funds.

**Problem solved:** Most trading bots are slow, custodial, or too complex. HawkX combines professional-grade features with a clean, mobile-first UI — and you always hold your own keys.

**Why Solana?** Solana's speed and low fees make it the ideal chain for high-frequency trading bots. With sub-second finality and near-zero transaction costs, HawkX can execute trades faster than any other chain.

  keyboards.js           — All UI menus & buttons
  executor.js            — Trade execution engine

  notifications.js       — All notifications
  ranks.js               — Rank system + cron
